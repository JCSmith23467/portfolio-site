Planetary Society of America Click to reveal details

Utilizes a wide range of HTML elements, CSS properties, and JavaScript programming to form a comprehensive website dedicated to astronomy and space exploration.

  • Main homepage introducing the Planetary Society of America, featuring navigation links to various sections and a slideshow of astronomical images.
  • A page dedicated to providing the latest alerts on astronomical events such as comet approaches, asteroid passes, magnetic pole shifts, and solar wind events. It includes a script to dynamically display the current date.
  • A contact page where users can fill out a form to contact the Planetary Society, including fields for personal details and a section for comments or questions.
  • An educational page that offers basic information on astronomy, descriptions of famous telescopes, and biographies of historical astronomers. It aims to educate both adults and children about astronomy.
  • A registration page for seminars offered by the Planetary Society. It includes a form for users to sign up for seminars, calculate costs, and details on different events.
  • A shopping page where users can browse and purchase products related to astronomy, such as binoculars, telescopes, books, and educational materials. It includes a form for selecting products and calculating the total cost of the order.
